Motion, dialed up — pick by letter.

Same identity as the locked kit: ultramarine signature, warm steel, sharp corners, one sans. Only the motion changes. Every element below is rendered in several labeled variants — hover, click, and replay them, then tell me which letters to fold in (e.g. Button B, Card E, Ignite C).

One rule held even here: no drop shadows. Every "lift" is transform plus a hairline and a tone step, never a shadow — that restraint is most of why the kit reads premium rather than templated. If a variant ever feels too much, that's the signal it's not for this brand.
