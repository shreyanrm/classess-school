A daily practice for the mind — mastery built through retrieval and independent attempts, not passive watching. A ritual, not a cram.
How does the daily budget work?
It meters concepts mastered, not minutes spent. The limit loosens early and tightens as you grow — and never bites before the aha.
Is my child's data safe?
Identity is vaulted and kept separate from behaviour. Nothing is sold. Deep profiling only switches on at the consent tier the law permits.
