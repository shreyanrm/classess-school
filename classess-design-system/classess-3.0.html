01 — Foundations

The idea

Classess looks like nothing else in its category because it refuses the category's instincts. Edtech shouts: red badges, confetti, urgency, noise. We do the opposite — spacious, certain, quiet — and let the intelligence underneath do the work. The restraint is not an absence of power; it is the signal of it.

The whole system reduces to one image: steel and one hit of pigment. Warm graphite and warm white carry almost everything; a single ultramarine does the meaning. Depth is drawn with hairlines, never shadows. Corners are sharp. Type is one quiet grotesque doing all the work through weight. premium is the message, not the decoration

05 — Foundations

The laws

Ten rules settle every unclear decision. They are why the system stays coherent across a styleguide, a motion language, and two hundred components.

01

Steel and one hit of pigment

Warm graphite and warm white carry the surface. A single vivid does the meaning. Never two vivids competing in one view.

02

No shadows, ever

Depth is a hairline, a tonal step, or frost on an overlay. Nothing floats on a blur.

03

Color is semantic, never decorative

If a color appears, it carries a subject or a state. Decorative color is noise, and noise reads cheap.

04

Ultramarine is the signature

It belongs to the brand and to the moment of mastery. It is never assigned to a subject.

05

One typeface, hierarchy by weight

A single grotesque, sized and weighted. No serif, no second family, no decoration through type.

06

Sharp corners

0–3px. Pills only where a control is genuinely round. Softness is borrowed from elsewhere; we don't borrow.

07

Whitespace is the luxury

Air is deliberate and generous. Density is the category's tell; space is ours.

08

Calm on the surface, intensity underneath

The interface never shouts, even while the system does something genuinely hard. Calm is a status signal.

09

Motion animates meaning, never chrome

A misconception shatters, scaffolding fades, a derivation assembles. Motion explains; it never just decorates.

10

Premium is load-bearing

The expensive feel is not vanity. Processing fluency makes learning feel achievable — the polish is a teaching tool.
